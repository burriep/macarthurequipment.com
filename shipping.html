Shipping and Returns

Placing Orders

For larger orders such as plows, we require a minimum of 50% down upon placing order. We’ll pull together the items and hold them for your arrival. The remaining 50% is due upon installation.

For special order parts that we do not stock, we require a minimum of 50% down at time of order. The balance is due at the time of pick-up.

Returns

Because most of the parts we offer are for specific vehicles, it is difficult to resell them. However, if you have ordered the wrong part, call us or stop in the parts department to discuss this further. We’ll do what we can to help you. If we are able to resell the product, we may accept it back with a 20% restocking fee.

Shipping

How and when your order is sent depends on the size of the item and how quickly you need it. In all cases, we will try to find the best rate for you. We charge the actual cost to ship, plus any incoming and outgoing fees charged to us.

Smaller packages are generally sent via UPS Ground to save you money, but can be sent next day air for your convenience at a higher UPS shipping rate

Larger packages are shipped by common carrier. We will contact several carriers to find the best rate. Immediate shipping is also available for a premium. Factory-to-direct orders incur a slightly higher cost. We will inform you of your shipping costs prior to sending your item.
